For airports, polygons are twice as nice!

Welcome to our Targeted Editing series where today you can figure out how to cook with thyme, or make OpenStreetMap data sublime! (both take about the same amount of time)

  1. How are airport polygons used?

    1. Help with map rendering! No, this doesn't mean cook the fat out of it, this means help with how things are cartographically displayed on the map. A map label is great to have, but a polygon is even better!
